The Problem

Normally all over the world the emmision of greenhouse gasses is defided by the gross domestic product (GPD) of a country. This presumes that rich countries are allowed to have more emmision than poor countries. this is not fair, in this project we will make a visualisation based on variables that exlude wealth and instead show a fair view on greenhouse gass emmision per country.

The Solution

in this visualisation we show the greenhouse gass emmisions per county devided by two factors: population and ratio between im- and export. We believe that every person in this world is allowed to create as much gass emmision as another since people have equal rights. This way a country who has to profide for a larger group should be able to have more greenhouse gass emmisions. However, some countries do make a lot for others and because of this the have more greenhouse gass emmisions since the other countries do not have to make it themselves. We try to correct this by defiding the import/export. This way countries who do their greenhouse gass emmisions in other countries will still be negetively influenced.
